Information Security Policy

This policy provides an advanced understanding of the concepts involved in firewalls, routers, intrusion detection, intrusion prevention and Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) in relationship to the overall enterprise network strategy. The policy outlines advanced network security installation techniques; advanced network security troubleshooting; and how to make intelligent choices in firewall and router technology. Additionally, the policy has a comprehensive look at the use of routers and switches with other network security components in configuring De-Militarized Zones (DMZ) and VPNs for optimal perimeter security.
